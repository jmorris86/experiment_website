Onlinemarket

Overview

You are a purchaser of goods in an online marketplace that consists of three different vendors. The goods sold by each vendor are exactly the same. There are ten rounds in total, and you are required to make one purchase in each round.

The vendors are unchanged in each round. However, their average rating is updated for their performance in the previous round.

Due to delivery issues in the market, orders sometimes arrive in bad condition. If the order arrives in bad condition it will be rated 1. Whereas, orders that arrive in good condition are rated as 5. Each 5 purchase is valued at £1, and each 1 is valued at £0.

Each vendor is randomly allocated a type, Good and Bad. Products arrive in good condition from Good vendors more often than from Bad vendors. However, at times, products arrive in bad condition from both types of vendor.

Given delivery is, outside the control of the vendors, if a product arrives in good condition in one round, it does not mean it will arrive in good condition in the following round, and vice versa.

Highlights

Your role

  • You are a purchaser in an online marketplace. In each round you will be asked to buy a product from one of several vendors.
  • Each purchase from a vendor generates payoff of £1 or £0.

Vendors

  • Vendors are real people who will be paid a percentage of their total sales that generated a payoff of £1.
  • They have been randomly selected as Good or Bad vendors.
  • Good vendors are more likely to pay £1 than bad vendors.
